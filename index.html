What is good design?

Effective design is attention-grabbing, easy to comprehend, and clearly communicated the message you're trying to get across -- whether that be social, commercial, political, or creative. I usually design out of a love for art, so I focus on the latter. I want my art and creative projects to be flashy and fun, but not too busy to be difficult to comprehend (unless confusion is the desired emotion I'm trying to evoke). Good design is completely dependent on the purpose of the design.
